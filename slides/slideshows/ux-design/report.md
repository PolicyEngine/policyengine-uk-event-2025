Biniam Gebre and MaSara Myers present the user experience research behind PolicyEngine 2.0. Through interviews with policymakers, researchers, journalists, and citizens, they gathered insights about needs and pain points. The speakers explain how these insights shaped design decisions, from information architecture to visual design, ensuring microsimulation results are both accurate and comprehensible. Gebre and Myers showcase design patterns for distributional analysis, budget impacts, and household-level effects.
