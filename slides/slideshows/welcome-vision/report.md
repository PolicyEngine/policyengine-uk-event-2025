Max Ghenis introduces PolicyEngine as a nonprofit providing free, open-source microsimulation tools for tax and benefit policy analysis. PolicyEngine UK is the UK's only open-source tax-benefit microsimulation model, built on OpenFisca and using the Family Resources Survey enhanced through data fusion and machine learning. Ghenis announces the Nuffield Foundation's December 2024 grant, enabling expansion with local authority datasets, childcare policy integration, validation dashboards, and platform improvements.
