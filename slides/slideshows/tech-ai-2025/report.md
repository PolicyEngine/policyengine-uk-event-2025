Nikhil Woodruff explores how PolicyEngine leverages artificial intelligence and modern software architecture to transform UK policy analysis. Building on the OpenFisca-Core framework, PolicyEngine has developed a technical stack that processes complex policy simulations in real-time, enabling users to receive instant feedback on reform proposals. Woodruff explains how PolicyEngine employs machine learning for data enhancement, including addressing the Family Resources Survey's known undersampling of high-income households.
